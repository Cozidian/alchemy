The 2024 United States presidential election, held on November 5, resulted in former President Donald Trump defeating incumbent Vice President Kamala Harris. Trump secured 312 electoral votes to Harris's 226, marking his return to the White House after a four-year absence. This victory makes Trump the second president in U.S. history, after Grover Cleveland, to serve non-consecutive terms.
